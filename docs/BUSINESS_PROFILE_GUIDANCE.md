# Business profile guidance

Create a Google Business Profile only if the business meets Google’s current eligibility rules for an in-person location or service-area operation. Do not create a virtual-office, lead-generation or fabricated local profile. The website’s global country pages do not establish local branches.

If eligible, use the verified legal/trading name, real customer-facing address or allowed service-area configuration, monitored phone/email, accurate hours, correct primary category and authentic facility/product photos. Keep ownership with the business, add managers rather than sharing credentials and document recovery access.

Ask genuine customers for unbiased reviews without incentives or gating. Respond factually and protect personal/order information. Keep profile products, services, URLs and claims synchronized with the verified website catalogue. If eligibility is uncertain, use Search Console and other legitimate business/trade profiles instead.
