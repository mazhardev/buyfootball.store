# 12-month content plan

Publish only after subject-matter review. Each item should answer a buyer question, link to relevant categories/services, disclose assumptions and avoid unsupported performance or certification claims.

| 7 | Futsal procurement for schools and clubs | Controlled rebound and indoor-use terminology |
| 8 | Packaging options for wholesale footballs | Carton planning and chargeable freight weight |
| 9 | International order timeline template | Production time versus transit time |
| 10 | Reorder planning for academy ball fleets | Recording wear without inventing durability claims |
| 11 | Questions to ask a football manufacturer | Documentation, testing and certification evidence |
| 12 | Annual buyer checklist and range review | What changed: verified product and policy updates |

Update existing guides when facts change. Prefer improving a strong canonical article to publishing multiple near-duplicates. Track qualified organic inquiries and assisted product/service actions, not just pageviews.
