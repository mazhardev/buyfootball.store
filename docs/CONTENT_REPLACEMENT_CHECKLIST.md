# Content replacement checklist

## Mandatory before launch

- [ ] Replace or verify every demonstration product name, ID, price, specification, stock status and MOQ.
- [ ] Approve or replace the five generated product visuals with rights-cleared photography and useful alternate views.
- [ ] Treat generated hero/category/factory/custom-process visuals as illustrative; use real brand and factory media for documentary claims.
- [ ] Add verified packaging, quality-control and custom-printing photography.
- [ ] Replace sample testimonials with permissioned customer content or remove the section.
- [ ] Decorative stars are present; connect them to genuine, displayed and eligible rating data before treating them as a customer rating.
- [ ] Verify factory story and every manufacturing/process description against actual operations.
- [ ] Verify all size, surface, care and product recommendations.
- [ ] Confirm certification/testing language product by product.
- [ ] Verify the configured email and WhatsApp number; replace any unconfigured social profiles and address details.
- [ ] Verify international shipping language and available destinations.
- [ ] Obtain legal review of privacy, cookies, returns and terms.
- [ ] Confirm each country page remains useful and materially unique.
- [ ] Review all blog articles with the product/manufacturing team.

## Image requirements

- [ ] Descriptive, lowercase, hyphenated filenames
- [ ] Modern compressed formats where practical
- [ ] Explicit dimensions and responsive sizes
- [ ] Accurate alt text; decorative images use empty alt text
- [ ] Rights records and model/property permissions retained
- [ ] No protected club, tournament or competitor marks without authorization
- [ ] 1200×630 social preview tested on common platforms

## Final copy pass

- [ ] No unverified “best”, “official”, “approved”, “certified” or professional-organization claim
- [ ] No fake scarcity, fake discount or invented review
- [ ] No local inventory/warehouse claim without operational evidence
- [ ] Prices clearly state inclusions/exclusions
- [ ] Contact and turnaround expectations are realistic
