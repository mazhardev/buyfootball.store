# International SEO

The canonical origin is `https://buyfootball.store`. Country pages are English market landing pages, not complete localized site versions. All eight are currently `noindex`, excluded from the sitemap and excluded from hreflang until a human market review is complete.

## Safe rollout

1. Confirm the market can actually be served, including shipping, returns, currency, support and importer obligations.
2. Assign a qualified native-language reviewer where a local-language page is promised.
3. Add material local value: terminology, buyer segments, shipping context, product obligations and useful FAQs. Do not only swap the country name.
4. Set `humanReviewed`, `translationComplete` and `indexable` only after approval.
5. Add self-referencing canonical and reciprocal hreflang only between genuinely equivalent, indexable pages. Use valid language-region codes and an `x-default` when an appropriate global selector/landing page exists.
6. Test exported alternate links and submit the new URLs through the sitemap.

The current Germany, France, Spain, Italy and Netherlands content remains English; it must not advertise itself as German, French, Spanish, Italian or Dutch. Currency display does not make a page localized. Never imply local inventory, a warehouse, local legal entity or included taxes unless verified.

If a market page cannot sustain unique value, consolidate it into the global shipping page rather than publishing a doorway page.
