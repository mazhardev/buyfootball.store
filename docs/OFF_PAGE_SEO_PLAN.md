# Off-page SEO plan

Earn references through real expertise and relationships; do not buy links or mass-submit spun content.

- Publish useful original resources that clubs, schools, retailers and sourcing teams can cite.
- Seek relevant supplier, chamber, trade-association and customer-partner listings only when membership/relationship is genuine.
- Provide expert commentary on football construction, specification and procurement with transparent attribution.
- Turn verified customer projects into permissioned case studies without exposing confidential designs or claims.
- Keep name, domain, email and business identity consistent across authoritative profiles.
- Monitor unlinked brand mentions and inaccurate listings; request corrections politely.
- Use campaign tracking on controlled outreach links without manipulating anchor text.

Reject link schemes, automated directories, private blog networks, paid dofollow placements and fake review campaigns. Evaluate success through qualified referral traffic, relevant earned domains, brand search and lead quality—not domain-rating scores alone.
