# Trust content checklist

## Business identity

- [ ] Legal/trading name, ownership, address and manufacturing relationship are verified.
- [x] Domain, email and WhatsApp are configured; owners must confirm monitoring.
- [ ] Social profiles are real, controlled and current.

## Products and operations

- [ ] Every name, image, price, comparison price, specification, variant, MOQ and availability is approved per SKU.
- [ ] Factory, testing, quality and certification claims have retained evidence.
- [ ] Shipping, duties, production time, returns and payment expectations match operations.
- [ ] Generated images are replaced or clearly approved as illustration—not evidence.

## Customers and proof

- [x] Sample testimonials are removed.
- [x] Decorative stars remain aria-hidden as requested and are not represented as rating data.
- [ ] Future reviews have permission, provenance and an honest moderation policy.
- [ ] Case studies disclose what was supplied and avoid confidential or unsupported results.

## Editorial and legal

- [ ] A named business subject-matter owner reviews commercial/manufacturing content.
- [ ] Qualified advisers review privacy, cookies, terms, returns and target-market obligations.
- [ ] Articles have real publish/update dates, a review owner and a change record.
- [ ] Corrections and customer complaints have documented routes and owners.
